Why Servant Leadership?

Servant leadership is a Christ-centered approach where the leader serves first. Jesus modeled this in John 13 when He washed the disciples’ feet, showing that greatness in God’s Kingdom comes through humility and love.

In our dorms, classrooms, and communities, this posture changes how we organize, communicate, and care for people. Leading like Jesus means elevating others, stewarding influence wisely, and keeping the Gospel at the center of our work.

Core Principles

  • Humility before God and people
  • Love expressed through action
  • Integrity in private and public
  • Stewardship of time, gifts, and resources

First Steps This Week

  1. Pray and ask the Holy Spirit where to serve one person today.
  2. Offer practical help (carry, clean, tutor, encourage).
  3. Follow up with care and consistency.

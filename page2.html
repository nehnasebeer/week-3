Daily Habits

Begin your day with prayer and Scripture, inviting the Lord to shape your schedule. Ask, “Who can I serve today?” Then look for small openings: holding a door, sharing notes, or giving someone your full attention.

Build rhythms that bless others: tidy shared spaces, volunteer for the quiet tasks, and celebrate teammates. These practices prepare your heart for larger leadership moments because faithfulness in little things leads to more (Luke 16:10).

Micro‑Acts of Service

  • Encourage a classmate with a sincere note or text.
  • Start a 10‑minute clean‑up after group meetings.
  • Share resources (study guides, food pantry info, campus ministries).
  • Offer prayer respectfully when invited.
